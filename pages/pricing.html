FAQ

Check out some of the questions we frequently get asked.
Does Storemia support variants ?
Yes! Storemia allows you to add items with different variants such as colors, sizes and other custom variants. You can even add different prices for different variant
Do I get charged from my sales ?
No! Storemia doesn't charge you for any sales income generated. All the money you make is yours. You only select a subscription package to access Storemia features
Can I Charge Delivery Fees ?
Yes! Storemia allows you to add delivery locations and associated fees that will be added up to customer's total cost when checking out.
Can I run Ads With My Website ?
Yes! You can use your shop url for Facebook and Instagram ads. You can even Integrate your Storemia store items with Facebook catalogue items which can be used in Facebook shop, Instagram shop and Facebook marketplace
